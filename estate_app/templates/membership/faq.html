Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I list a property?

To list a property:

  1. Sign up for a seller account
  2. Choose a membership plan
  3. Click "List Property" in your dashboard
  4. Fill in property details and upload photos
  5. Submit for review (typically approved within 24 hours)

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods through Razorpay:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Net Banking (All major Indian banks)
  • UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.)
  • Digital Wallets
  • EMI Options

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time:

  • Upgrades: Take effect immediately with prorated billing
  • Downgrades: Take effect at the end of your current billing cycle

You'll receive credit for any unused portion when upgrading.

How long does property approval take?

Property listings are typically reviewed and approved within:

  • Standard review: 24-48 hours
  • Priority review (Premium plans): 4-6 hours

We review listings for:

  • Complete information
  • Clear, appropriate photos
  • Accurate pricing
  • Compliance with our guidelines

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

When you cancel:

  • You continue to have access until the end of your billing period
  • Your active listings become inactive
  • You can no longer create new listings
  • Your data is preserved for 30 days
  • You can reactivate anytime

Note: No refunds are provided for partial months.
