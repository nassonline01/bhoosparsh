Frequently Asked Questions

Account verification is a simple 3-step process:

  1. Email Verification: Click the verification link sent to your email
  2. Phone Verification: Enter the OTP sent to your mobile number
  3. Document Verification: Upload your PAN/Aadhar card for seller verification

Note: Verified accounts receive 3x more trust from buyers.

Photo limits depend on your subscription plan:

  • Basic Plan: 10 photos per listing
  • Premium Plan: 20 photos per listing
  • Pro Plan: 30 photos per listing
  • Enterprise Plan: 50 photos per listing

Pro Tip: Properties with 8+ photos get 4x more views than those with fewer photos.
